Our Background


What began as a small research circle in 1924 has evolved into a premier institution. We bridge the gap between classical education and the digital age.

1924


Foundation

Founded by Dr. Alistair Thorne with a vision for critical inquiry and structural linguistics.

1968


Global Expansion

Inauguration of the International Research Wing, hosting scholars from six continents.

Our Mission

To cultivate a community of independent thinkers who leverage the power of language, logic, and ethical inquiry to solve the world's most complex challenges.

Our vision

To be the global gold standard for editorialized education, where information is transformed into profound wisdom.

Message from our Principal


Dr Hassan Sankoh

At The Academic Editorial, we believe that education is not merely the transmission of facts, but the curation of a mindset. In an era of overflowing information, our role is to teach students how to filter, analyze, and articulate truth. Our faculty members are not just teachers; they are mentors and practicing scholars who guide students through the labyrinth of modern thought with a steady hand and a critical eye.
